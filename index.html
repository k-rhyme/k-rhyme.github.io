Thank you for coming to my web page. My name is Karim Moussalli and I am half Swiss and half Egyptian. My passions are computers, TV series, and travel.
I lived in Egypt (Cairo) up to the age of ten and completed my kindergarten and primary school at the British International School Cairo. In 1995 I moved in Switzerland with my family. More precisely in the italian spoken part of Switzerland (Ticino).
In 1999 I've completed my middle school at the Scuola Media Riva San Vitale and in 2003 I've finished my high school at the liceo Elvetico of Lugano.
Since one of my passions was and still is computers (and any kind of new technology), in 2004 I decided to do my bachelor in computer sciences at the Università della Svizzera italiana in Lugano.
Once I have completed my bachelor, I started my working experiance in 2007 as a web developer in a company that created web sites for other companies. After a few years, I became the project manager of the company and my tasks were to coordinate different teams of web developers, both in Switzerland and abroad, to manage various web sites for our clients.
Since I liked the work experience as a project manager, in 2013 I decided to do a master in Management and Informatics at the Università della Svizzera italiana in Lugano.
Nowadays I am working as a full-stack developer, my favorite programming language is PHP, but since I learned different programming languages, I have no difficulty in learning new ones easily.
